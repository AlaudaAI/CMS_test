Manifest / Profile

I am a multidisciplinary designer and developer operating at the intersection of systematic design and functional technology. With over eight years of experience building digital products, I approach each project as an exercise in structured problem-solving — reducing complexity to its essential components.

My practice centers on design systems, interface architecture, and typographic precision. I believe that the most effective digital experiences emerge from rigorous constraint and deliberate reduction rather than decorative accumulation. Every element must justify its existence within the system.

Previously, I have contributed to projects spanning enterprise software, independent publishing platforms, and architectural visualization tools. My work has been recognized for its technical rigor and systematic clarity, earning acknowledgment from the Swiss Style Awards and the International Typographic Society.

I operate from Berlin and am currently accepting select commissions for systems-level design work, brand identity development, and technical consulting engagements. If your project demands precision and structural integrity, I would be glad to discuss collaboration.
